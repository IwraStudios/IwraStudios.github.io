So I think now is a good time to tell about myself. I’m Ryan and a few years back I was a shy, caring boy, who loved life and all people around it. I cared about everything and everyone, whilst still too afraid to talk out in the open. I did not have a lot of friends, and mostly at school I didn’t have the guts to talk. I was that guy who you mostly forget is even in your class, he is too normal to be a geek or cool. A quiet boy who you only ask for a pen, but not much more and sometimes not even that. Also I didn’t like the way the normal guys in our class went about, too violent for my taste. And because I’m raised a bunch by my sister and nieces, I see woman in a different perspective, rather than just their bodies. That made me different in their eyes, but the girls where actually easier for me to talk to anyways. I think that they talked back was because I was maybe pathetic but now I actually think they do it because of a gay-friend complex or so. But now you know a little about me in that time so…
