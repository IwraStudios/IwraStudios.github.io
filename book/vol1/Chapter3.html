Chapter III

Around that moment I got home, settled my things on the table like I usually do and sat down on the piano stool. I always like to sit on the piano stool. Not only because it’s in the middle of the kitchen but also I just like playing the piano. Around that time I was still learning to play freely without any music-paper. I could do a little improvisation, but not further than every sad song ever. Usually I play classical music to impress people like parents and such. They always go crazy if you can play Bach or Fur Elíse on the piano like it’s nothing. Not that I did not like classical music, which I still do. But when you learn pop music on the piano, which most people do, it will be boring in 2 or 3 weeks. And to perfect my piano movements for a song that is just for 2 or 3 weeks is a little bit of a waste.

Usually I’d get some food to go with chatting on the piano stool, like a sandwich or chips, but now I was too distracted to do anything.

(Unknown Caller)

Okay
I’m going to bedss

Ah well

Yea I’m tired
Bye

Byes

I laid down my phone and got up. Opened the cabinet and poured myself a glass of water. Whilst sipping on my glass played some piano. Although muffles piano didn’t sound to good, it was good to get some practise. A good half hour passed and my glass was as good as empty. I opened my phone for the last updates on the messages I got. 6.

Heyyyy duuidree
Wherte did yuu goooooo??/?

Drunk ass friends of me always types like he has rhino fingers when he is not from this world anymore.

Go home man, you’re drunk

Next message was from my mom.

Are you home already?
I’ll be home tomorrow or so.
Turning off my phone now

My mom was never home and I’m still always home alone. Most people think it’s sad that she is never home, but I always think it’s peaceful to have nobody home. It’s because then you can do whatever you want whenever you want to. At least that is what I’ve always told everybody then, to let them think I do crazy things, but I mostly like it because I used to talk to myself all the time when I would be alone. Mostly English and out loud. If you would be there you’d think I was mental.

Yea mom, I’m home safe

See you then, have fun!

And the last message got me confused a lot. It was from the unknown person.

See ya tomorrow

That message confused and shocked me at the same time. The next day was the last day free from school. I replied simply with:

Tomorrow??”

Hoping on a reply, I received nothing back. Which was to be expected as it was already past 1 a clock in the morning. I made myself ready for bed, brushing my teeth, drinking a last cup of water and what not. Whilst reviewing my day, I thought to myself that I had a pretty eventful day. Going to a party, meeting someone new on the internet and bumping in to a person literally leaving an impression. Back then I used to have pretty boring days around those times, because I didn’t go out much or had many adventurous friends to take me out. A peaceful way of living is what I’d always say. Back then I thought it was a great way of living. The last couple of things I thought were peaceful, that person and why I didn’t just ask a simple name from him, my nice time at the party which was introvert- anti-social but still nice and thoughts of that girl I bumped in to and hoping that I would see her again.

“But what is there tomorrow?”
