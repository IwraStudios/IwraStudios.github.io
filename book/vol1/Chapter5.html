Chapter V

And there it was, a message I was expecting a long time ago.I thought it was funny that he didn’t ask my name when we met, but that would be the magic of the internet and anonymity. Clearly not.

(Unknown Caller)



































On that moment my phone screen turned black, then white and afterwards it turned off. “What!?” I screamed out loud, “What the hell just happened”. I ran upstairs for my charger, plugged it in the nearest electric socket I could find and tried pushing every button to see if that gave any reaction. Nothing. When I finally gave up I sat down onto the couch and turned on my computer. I opened a online chat and messaged my technical friend.

Casper

















And then I realised that last night I did indeed drop my phone on the pavement, and that that could be the reason why it won’t start up again.
















He send me a link, I opened it and it showed a web page with a new phone which wasn’t all to expensive. I opened my bank account to see if there were any funds left, because I didn’t have a job at the time. “Yes” I thought to myself, “Just a couple hundred, that will do” I transferred a small sum to my pay account and opened the chat again.















I opened the internet page again and added the phone to “My Cart”. Normally I would hesitate when buying a product above fifty, but not this time, this time it was different. I was actually to curious about that person to just let it go. And furious as I was for my phone letting me down on one of the worst time ever, I did my best to control my purchases to a logical product. “So that is done, hopefully tomorrow it will be delivered”.
